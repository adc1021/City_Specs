Welcome to City Specs!

• City Specs displays data for cities across the United States

• The bar graph provides listing prices for homes that have a property size of roughly 2500 sq ft.

• The chart to the right displays scores for different urban areas provided by the Teleport API. Each score is out of 10. 1 being a poor performer and 10 being excellent.

• If you'd like to view any of these homes, just click on the button titled "More Info" above the chart

• On the map, click once to zoom into the marker for a specific city

• double-click the same marker to see an image of the home
